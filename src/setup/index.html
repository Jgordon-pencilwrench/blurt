Welcome to Blurt

A few things need to be set up before you start.

🤖
Ollama (local AI)
Blurt uses Ollama to summarise your voice notes locally, with no data leaving your machine.
🎙️
Microphone access
Blurt needs microphone access to record your voice.
⌨️
Accessibility (auto-type)
To type text directly at your cursor, Blurt needs Accessibility permission. Without it, text is copied to your clipboard instead.
